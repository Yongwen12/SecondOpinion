Terms of use

A sharp tool still needs a guardrail: verify sources, keep people anonymous, and do not turn a score into a personal verdict.

Use of the service

Judge My Reviewers is an independent research and commentary tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by OpenReview or the listed venues. You may browse public scorecards and use optional account features for lawful, good-faith purposes.

One important boundary

Scores evaluate review text, not reviewers as people. Do not use the service to identify anonymous reviewers, harass people, coordinate retaliation, publish private information, or make unsupported allegations.

AI output

Scores, labels, summaries, and rankings are generated or transformed by automated systems and may be incomplete, biased, or wrong. They are not academic, employment, legal, or reputational determinations. Verify every excerpt against its linked OpenReview source before relying on it.

Accounts and availability

Keep credentials secure and provide accurate registration information. We may rate-limit, suspend, or remove access used for abuse or interference. The service is provided as available, without a promise that scores or access will be uninterrupted, error-free, or fit for a particular purpose.

Disputes and takedowns

Use the score-report form for incorrect scoring, missing context, attribution, privacy, or takedown concerns. Include the source URL and a concise explanation. For personal-data requests, email privacy@smartselling.work.
